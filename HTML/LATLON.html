Program LATLON

Usage 

    setenv  GRIDDESC      <path name>
    setenv  <gridfile>    <path name>
    setenv  <bndyfile>    <path name>
    
    latlon <and respond to the prompts>

Summary

Construct matching time independent one-layer GRIDDED and BOUNDARY files with variables LAT and LON containing the latitude and longitude at cell centers, for a user specified coordinate system and grid. Specifications for this grid may either come from a GRIDDESC file or may be entered interactively.

Currently, only Lat-lon, Lambert, UTM, Mercator, Stereographic, and Albers Equal-Area coordinate systems for interactive input, and boundaries with NTHIK > 0 are supported. You may turn off either output file (GRIDDED or BOUNDARY) by responding "NONE" to the prompt for its name.
